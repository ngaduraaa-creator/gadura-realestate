About Nitin Gadura

Nitin Gadura is a licensed New York State Real Estate Salesperson at Gadura Real Estate LLC, the family-owned brokerage founded in 2006 by Vinod K. Gadura at 106-09 101st Ave in Ozone Park, Queens. Nitin was born and raised in Queens and has worked in NYC + Long Island residential real estate for more than 7 years. He has personally closed over $100 million in transactions and represented more than 500 families across Queens, Brooklyn, The Bronx, Manhattan, Staten Island, Nassau County, and Suffolk County.

Nitin's specialty is helping immigrant and first-generation American families navigate the New York homebuying process — particularly within the Indo-Caribbean, Guyanese, South Asian, Punjabi, Sikh, Bengali, and Hispanic communities of Queens and Long Island. He speaks English, Hindi, Punjabi, Gujarati, Urdu, Bengali, and Guyanese Creole, which lets him communicate directly with multi-generational families during the highest-stakes financial decision of their lives.

His professional reputation is built on three things: deep neighborhood knowledge of every Queens and Long Island corridor, transparency around the true costs of buying and selling in New York (including realistic net-sheets, accurate property-tax estimates, and unbiased lender comparisons), and patient guidance for first-time buyers — especially those navigating FHA, SONYMA, HomeReady, and VA loan programs.
