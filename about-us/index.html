Our Story

18+ Years Helping Queens Families Buy & Sell

Gadura Real Estate LLC was founded in 2006 by Vinod Gadura, a licensed Broker with over 20 years of real estate experience across Queens, Nassau, Suffolk, and Brooklyn. What started as a passion for helping families find their dream homes has grown into a full-service brokerage trusted by hundreds of clients.

Our office is located in the heart of Ozone Park, Queens — a neighborhood we know and love. We serve the diverse communities of South Queens including Ozone Park, Howard Beach, Richmond Hill, South Ozone Park, Jamaica, and beyond.

At Gadura Real Estate, we believe in building lasting relationships. Every client receives personal attention, honest market analysis, and relentless advocacy from start to close.
