About

A Family Brokerage. A Block-Level Broker.

Nitin Gadura is a licensed New York State real estate salesperson with Gadura Real Estate, LLC — the family-owned brokerage his father Vinod K. Gadura founded in 2006. Nitin works buyers, sellers, and investors across Queens, Brooklyn, Nassau, and Suffolk Counties, with specific depth in Ozone Park, Howard Beach, Richmond Hill, South Richmond Hill, Jamaica, Queens Village, and the Nassau border corridor including Valley Stream, Elmont, and Floral Park.

Growing up in Ozone Park and working the family business for over a decade gives Nitin a rare combination: street-level knowledge of South Queens neighborhoods and modern data-driven pricing discipline. Every listing gets documented OneKey® MLS sold comps, honest feedback, and a written commission up front. Every buyer gets access to off-market inventory through the Gadura network and a careful review of every contract term.

The team works in English, Hindi, Punjabi, Bengali, Urdu, Gujarati, and Spanish — important in Queens, the most linguistically diverse county in America. No ghostwriters and no SEO mills: every blog post on this site is written by Nitin himself.
