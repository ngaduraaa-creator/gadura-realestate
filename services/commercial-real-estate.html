Commercial Real Estate in Queens, Brooklyn & Long Island

Nitin Gadura | Gadura Real Estate, LLC | Your Commercial Property Expert

Expert Commercial Real Estate Services

The commercial real estate landscape across Queens, Brooklyn, and Long Island presents extraordinary opportunities for business owners, entrepreneurs, and investors. From bustling retail corridors along Queens Boulevard and Jamaica Avenue to emerging office markets in Long Island City and mixed-use developments throughout Nassau County, this region offers some of the most dynamic commercial property markets in the greater New York area.

Nitin Gadura of Gadura Real Estate, LLC provides hands-on commercial real estate services tailored to your business objectives. Whether you are searching for a retail storefront to launch your business, an office suite to accommodate your growing team, a warehouse or industrial facility for operations, or a mixed-use property that combines commercial income with residential units, Nitin delivers the local market knowledge and deal-making expertise that drives results.

Types of Commercial Properties We Handle

  • Retail Spaces: Storefronts, shopping centers, and restaurant spaces along high-traffic corridors in Queens, Brooklyn, and Long Island towns. From standalone buildings to units within commercial strips, we match your business with the right location and foot traffic.
  • Office Properties: Professional office suites, entire floors, and office buildings suited to law firms, medical practices, tech companies, financial services, and other professional tenants. Locations near courthouses, transit hubs, and commercial centers.
  • Mixed-Use Buildings: Properties combining ground-floor retail or commercial space with upper-floor residential units. These versatile assets generate multiple income streams and are highly sought after by investors throughout the NYC metro area.
  • Industrial & Warehouse: Distribution centers, light manufacturing facilities, and warehouse space in Queens and Long Island's industrial zones. Proximity to JFK, LaGuardia, and major highways makes this region prime for logistics operations.
  • Development Sites: Vacant land and teardown properties zoned for commercial or mixed-use development. Nitin helps developers identify parcels with the right zoning, access, and growth potential.

Commercial Real Estate Hot Spots

Several areas within our service territory stand out for commercial investment and business opportunities:

  • Long Island City: Queens' fastest-growing commercial district with modern office towers, co-working spaces, and retail opportunities near the waterfront.
  • Jamaica, Queens: Major transit hub experiencing significant commercial redevelopment, with new retail, dining, and office projects transforming the downtown area.
  • Flushing, Queens: One of the busiest commercial districts in the outer boroughs, with strong demand for retail, restaurant, and office space serving a large Asian-American population.
  • Astoria, Queens: Vibrant dining and retail scene along Broadway and Steinway Street, with growing demand for commercial space from restaurants, boutiques, and professional services.
  • Garden City, Long Island: Premium office and retail market anchored by Roosevelt Field Mall and professional office parks.
