What You Need to Rent in NYC

Preparing to rent in Queens or Long Island? Here's what most landlords require:

  • Proof of income: Pay stubs showing income of 40x the monthly rent (or a guarantor)
  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Credit report (most landlords accept 650+ credit scores)
  • Employment verification letter
  • References from previous landlords
  • Security deposit (typically one month's rent)
  • Broker fee (if applicable)

Don't meet all criteria? Ask me about guarantor services and alternative qualification options.
