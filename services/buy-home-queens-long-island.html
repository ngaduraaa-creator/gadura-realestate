First-Time Buyer Programs

New York offers several programs to help first-time buyers:

  • SONYMA low-interest mortgage programs
  • HomeFirst Down Payment Assistance (up to $100,000 for eligible NYC buyers)
  • FHA loans with as little as 3.5% down
  • VA loans for eligible veterans (0% down)
  • NYC Housing Development Corporation programs

Nitin Gadura will help you identify which programs you qualify for and connect you with the right lenders.
