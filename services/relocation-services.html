Moving to New York? Let a Local Expert Guide You

Relocating to a new city is one of life's biggest transitions — and moving to the New York metropolitan area presents unique challenges that out-of-town buyers simply cannot navigate alone. From understanding wildly different neighborhood dynamics to managing complex NYC closing costs and co-op board approvals, the learning curve is steep. I'm Nitin Gadura of Gadura Real Estate, LLC, and I provide comprehensive relocation services for individuals, families, and corporate transferees moving to Queens, Brooklyn, and Long Island.

Whether you are relocating from another state, another country, or from Manhattan to the outer boroughs, I serve as your local guide — helping you choose the right neighborhood, find the right home, and settle into your new community with confidence. My clients relocate from across the United States and internationally, including India, China, South Korea, the Caribbean, and South America.

What Our Relocation Services Include

  • Neighborhood Consultation: A detailed assessment of your priorities — commute, schools, budget, lifestyle, cultural amenities, and community character — matched against the best neighborhoods in our service area. Browse our neighborhood guides for an introduction.
  • Virtual & In-Person Tours: For clients who cannot visit before their move, I conduct video-call neighborhood tours and home walkthroughs so you can evaluate options remotely before booking a trip.
  • School District Research: Detailed information on public school ratings, charter options, private school proximity, and specialized programs to help families with school-age children choose the right location.
  • Commute Planning: Analysis of subway, LIRR, bus, and driving routes to your workplace. I help you understand realistic commute times and transportation costs from each target neighborhood.
  • Rental-to-Purchase Strategy: If you are not ready to buy immediately, I help you secure a rental in your target neighborhood while you acclimate, then transition to a home purchase when the time is right.
  • Temporary Housing Coordination: Connections to short-term furnished rentals and corporate housing for the transition period between arrival and permanent housing.
  • Local Services Network: Introductions to trusted attorneys, mortgage lenders, insurance agents, movers, utility providers, and other professionals you will need during your relocation.

Popular Relocation Destinations

Each area within our service territory attracts different types of relocating buyers:

  • Astoria, Queens: Young professionals and international transplants love its dining scene, cultural diversity, proximity to Midtown Manhattan (under 20 minutes on the N/W), and vibrant nightlife.
  • Forest Hills, Queens: Families relocating for top-rated schools, tree-lined streets, and a quiet suburban feel with express subway service to Manhattan.
  • Flushing, Queens: A global destination for Asian-American families, with excellent schools, unmatched culinary options, and a large, welcoming community.
  • Long Island City, Queens: Corporate relocations and professionals seeking luxury waterfront living with a one-stop subway ride to Midtown Manhattan.
  • Park Slope & Brooklyn Heights, Brooklyn: Families drawn to Brooklyn's best public schools, brownstone living, and proximity to Prospect Park.
  • Garden City & Great Neck, Long Island: Executives and families seeking premium suburban living with top-tier schools and a manageable LIRR commute.

Corporate Relocation Assistance

Nitin Gadura works with companies and HR departments to facilitate employee relocations. Our corporate relocation services include:

  • Dedicated point of contact for transferring employees and their families
  • Customized area orientation packages based on employee preferences and company location
  • Coordination with corporate relocation management companies and third-party providers
  • Streamlined home search process designed to minimize time away from work
  • Assistance with both rental and purchase transactions depending on the relocation timeline

International Relocation Expertise

Moving to New York from another country adds layers of complexity. Nitin Gadura assists international buyers with:

  • Understanding U.S. mortgage requirements for non-citizens and permanent residents
  • Navigating ITIN (Individual Taxpayer Identification Number) applications for buyers without Social Security numbers
  • Connecting with lenders who offer foreign national mortgage programs
  • Explaining property tax obligations, homeowner insurance requirements, and NYC-specific regulations
  • Multilingual support and cultural sensitivity throughout the transaction
