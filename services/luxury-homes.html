Luxury Neighborhoods We Serve

Our luxury real estate expertise spans the most prestigious communities in the region:

Queens Luxury Markets

  • Forest Hills: The landmark Forest Hills Gardens enclave features Tudor and Colonial homes on private, landscaped streets — one of the most exclusive residential communities in all of Queens.
  • Douglaston: Waterfront estates, the Douglaston Club, and grand homes on generous lots define this historic and affluent Queens neighborhood.
  • Bayside: Spacious Colonials, center-hall layouts, and manicured properties in one of Queens' most desirable family neighborhoods.
  • Long Island City: Penthouse condos with panoramic Manhattan skyline views and high-end waterfront residences in Queens' most modern luxury market.
  • Whitestone: Waterfront homes along the East River with private docks, sweeping views, and a serene suburban atmosphere.

Long Island Luxury Markets

  • Garden City: Tree-lined avenues, stately homes, and a walkable village center define one of Long Island's most prestigious addresses.
  • Great Neck: The inspiration for F. Scott Fitzgerald's East Egg, Great Neck features waterfront estates, grand Tudors, and luxury living on the Gold Coast.
  • Manhasset: Home to the Americana Manhasset luxury shopping destination, with elegant estates and top-rated schools on the North Shore.
  • Port Washington: Picturesque harbor setting with waterfront properties, express LIRR service, and a charming village center.
  • Roslyn: Historic charm meets modern luxury with beautifully renovated estates and new construction in a highly acclaimed school district.

What Defines a Luxury Property?

Luxury real estate in our market is distinguished by several key characteristics that set these properties apart:

  • Premium locations in established, prestigious neighborhoods with strong architectural character
  • Superior construction quality, high-end finishes, and custom architectural details
  • Generous lot sizes, landscaped grounds, and outdoor living amenities
  • Proximity to top-rated schools, country clubs, waterfront access, or cultural destinations
  • Modern amenities including smart home technology, chef's kitchens, spa-like bathrooms, and private outdoor spaces
