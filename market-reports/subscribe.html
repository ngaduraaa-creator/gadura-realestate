Get NY Market Reports in Your Inbox

Pick the ZIP codes that matter to you. On the 1st of every month, you'll get a short email with median prices, days on market, notable sales, reasons to buy, and a link to the full report. Written by Nitin Gadura, not by a bot.

Our promise: No spam. No selling your email. One email per month per ZIP you pick. Unsubscribe in one click any time. No pressure to list or buy.

How the Reports Work

  1. Written monthly. Nitin compiles MLS sold data from the prior month for each ZIP and writes a short summary.
  2. Sent on the 1st. You get one email per ZIP you picked on the 1st of every month.
  3. Short and scannable. 4–6 data points plus 1–2 paragraphs of "what it means." No 2,000-word fluff.
  4. Full report linked. Every email links to the web version, which carries the full reasons-to-buy content plus live MLS listings for that ZIP.
  5. Unsubscribe instantly. Every email carries a one-click unsubscribe footer.

What You'll Get in the Email

  • Median sale price — prior month vs. prior year
  • Days on market and months of supply
  • Sale-to-list ratio (are buyers paying asking, over, or under?)
  • Any notable closings or new-construction listings
  • Nitin's one-paragraph take: "here's what this means for buyers/sellers this month"
  • Link to the full web report with live MLS listings
