Why Buy in South Ozone Park

  1. Most active 2-family investor market in South Queens — deep inventory
  2. Strong rental demand from JFK workforce and Queens-Brooklyn commuters
  3. A train access via Aqueduct Racetrack–North Conduit Ave or Rockaway Blvd
  4. FHA 2–4 unit friendly for owner-occupant first-time buyers
  5. Consistent price growth over the last decade
  6. Diverse community including South Asian, Indo-Caribbean, and Hispanic families
  7. Relatively affordable entry into Queens ownership

Neighborhood Profile

Housing stock: 2-family investor submarket, some 1-family

School district: NYC DOE District 27

County: Queens

Things to Verify Before Buying

  • Older electrical and plumbing common in the housing stock
  • Some blocks near JFK experience aircraft noise
