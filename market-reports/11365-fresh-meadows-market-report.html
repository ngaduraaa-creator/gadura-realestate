Why Buy in Fresh Meadows

  1. District 26 schools — top-rated NYC public school zoning
  2. Large garden-apartment co-op inventory with park-like grounds
  3. Queens College nearby — steady rental demand
  4. Fresh Meadows Shopping Center and restaurants
  5. Detached single-families with yard space for families
  6. Easy access to both Grand Central Parkway and LIE
  7. Long-hold owner community — stable, quiet streets

Neighborhood Profile

Housing stock: Postwar detached 1-family, garden-apartment co-ops, some condos

School district: NYC DOE District 26

County: Queens

Things to Verify Before Buying

  • Longer subway commute — no direct subway service; buses to 7 train
  • Many co-op buildings have land-lease arrangements — review carefully
  • Some postwar detached homes need electrical and plumbing modernization
