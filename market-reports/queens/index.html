Frequently asked questions

What areas in Queens does Gadura Real Estate cover with market reports?

Gadura Real Estate publishes monthly market reports for three Queens ZIP codes: Howard Beach (11414), Ozone Park (11416), and Richmond Hill (11418). Each report includes the Zillow Home Value Index, year-over-year change, rental data, and mortgage rate context.

How often are the Queens market reports updated?

Reports are published monthly, aligned with the Zillow Research ZHVI release cycle. Each month a new report is generated for every covered ZIP code with updated home values, trend analysis, and charts.

Where does the data in these reports come from?

All home-value figures use the Zillow Home Value Index (ZHVI). Rental data uses the Zillow Observed Rent Index (ZORI). Mortgage rates come from Freddie Mac PMMS via FRED. Transaction data references the NYC Department of Finance Rolling Sales dataset. Every number links to its public source.
