Why Buy in Valley Stream

  1. First Long Island town off the Queens border — most accessible Nassau entry
  2. Strong LIRR service — 30-minute ride to Penn
  3. Green Acres Mall and commercial corridor
  4. Diverse, family-oriented community
  5. Relatively accessible Nassau pricing for detached homes
  6. Access to Long Island beaches and parks
  7. Solid school options across multiple districts
