Why Buy in Hempstead

  1. Nassau County seat — historic, central location
  2. Most accessible detached-home pricing in Nassau's inner ring
  3. LIRR Hempstead station with Main Line service
  4. Hofstra University and NuHealth medical anchor major employers
  5. Diverse, long-established community
  6. Active redevelopment and revitalization efforts
  7. Central Nassau location — everything else is a short drive
