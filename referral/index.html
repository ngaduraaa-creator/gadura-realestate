Referral Program FAQ

How much do I earn per referral?

$500 gift card per referral that results in a closed transaction. There's no limit — refer as many people as you'd like.

Do I need a real estate license?

No! Our referral program is for anyone — friends, family, colleagues, neighbors. You do NOT need a real estate license.

When do I get paid?

Your $500 gift card is sent within 7 days of closing. We'll email you a confirmation when the transaction closes.

What areas are covered?

All 5 NYC boroughs (Queens, Brooklyn, Bronx, Manhattan, Staten Island) plus Nassau County and Suffolk County on Long Island.

What if my friend is already working with an agent?

The referral must be someone not already in an active buyer/seller agreement with another agent. First-time introductions only.
