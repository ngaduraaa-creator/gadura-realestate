About Nitin

Queens & Long Island's Trusted Real Estate Specialist

Nitin Gadura is a Licensed NYS Real Estate Salesperson and a key agent at Gadura Real Estate, LLC — the full-service brokerage founded by Broker/Owner Vinod Gadura in 2006, based in Ozone Park, Queens.
