# Paid Advertising Playbook — Gadura Real Estate
## Google Ads + Facebook Ads + Retargeting + LSA
## Research completed: 2026-04-06

---

## KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY

### #1 PRIORITY: Google Local Service Ads (LSA)
- Appears ABOVE all other Google results — highest trust position
- Pay-per-LEAD not per-click — only pay when someone calls or messages
- Cost: $25–$75/lead (vs $200–$480 for Google Search PPC)
- $400/month gets 8–12 qualified leads
- Requires: background check, insurance proof, license, 5+ Google reviews
- **Setup: ads.google.com/local-services-ads**

---

## GOOGLE ADS

### Cost Reality (Queens/NYC)
- Average CPC: $8–$15+ per click (NYC premium)
- Cost per lead: $200–$480
- Budget needed: $1,000–$1,500/month for 25–30 leads

### Best Buyer Keywords
- "homes for sale in Queens NY"
- "houses for sale Ozone Park"
- "homes for sale Richmond Hill Queens"
- "realtor near me Queens NY"
- "buy a house Jamaica Queens"

### Best Seller Keywords (Higher value)
- "sell my house Queens NY"
- "what is my home worth Queens"
- "home value estimate Ozone Park"
- "sell my home fast Queens New York"

### Long-Tail Community Keywords (Lower CPC)
- "Indian real estate agent Queens NY"
- "Guyanese realtor Ozone Park"
- "Caribbean community homes for sale Richmond Hill"
- "best realtor for first time buyers Queens"

### Geo Targeting
- Queens zip codes: 11417, 11418, 11419, 11420, 11421, 11436
- South Nassau County
- Match types: Phrase + Exact ONLY (no broad match)
- Negative keywords: rental, apartment, for rent, zillow, jobs

## FACEBOOK/INSTAGRAM ADS

### Best Performing Formats
1. Video carousel — neighborhood + property walkthrough (70% more engagement)
2. Lead Gen Ads (native form — no landing page needed) — 9.70% conversion rate
3. Carousel — 3-5 properties or neighborhood angles
4. Single image — "What's Your Home Worth?" seller hook

### Buyer Targeting
- Age: 25–55, Queens + Nassau zip codes
- Interests: first-time home buyer, mortgage loans, Zillow, Trulia
- Life events: recently moved, newly engaged, expecting a child
- Behaviors: Likely to move

### Seller Targeting
- Age: 35–65
- Interests: home equity, home renovation, real estate investment
- Retarget: Past website visitors who viewed seller pages

### Budget & CPL
- Minimum: $1,000/month
- Consistent leads: $2,000–$3,000/month
- Expected CPL NYC: $35–$65 per lead

## RETARGETING (Highest ROI)

### Setup Requirements (DO FIRST)
1. Install Meta Pixel on gadurarealestate.com
2. Install Google Tag on gadurarealestate.com
3. Install Google Analytics 4

### Retargeting Segments
| Audience | Platform | Message |
|---|---|---|
| All website visitors (30 days) | Meta + Google Display | "Still looking in Queens?" |
| Visited seller/home value page | Meta | "Ready to list? Free CMA." |
| Visited listings pages | Google Display | New listings in same area |
| Spent 2+ min on site | Meta Lead Ad | Free consultation CTA |
| Watched 50%+ of video | Meta | Testimonial follow-up |

- Retargeted visitors: 70% more likely to convert
- Budget: $200–$400/month — punches above its weight
- Run retargeting window: 90 days minimum

---

## YOUTUBE ADS

- CPV: $0.03–$0.08 (very cheap impressions)
- Use as brand-building + retargeting layer (not primary lead gen)
- Budget: $300–$500/month
- Formats: Skippable in-stream, non-skippable 15-sec, bumper 6-sec
- Targeting: Custom intent audiences (people who searched real estate on Google)
