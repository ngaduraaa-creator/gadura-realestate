NYC HomeFirst Down Payment Assistance

NYC HomeFirst is a down payment assistance program offered by the NYC Department of Housing Preservation and Development (HPD) that provides up to $100,000 to eligible first-time homebuyers purchasing a 1-3 family home in New York City. The assistance is structured as a forgivable loan — if you remain in the home for 10-15 years, the loan is forgiven entirely. Income limits apply based on household size and area median income (AMI).

Additional Details

To qualify, you must complete a HUD-approved homebuyer education course, purchase a 1-3 family home in one of the five NYC boroughs, and meet income requirements.
