Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026  ·  Effective: April 4, 2026

1. Who We Are

Gadura Real Estate LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is a licensed real estate broker in New York State. Our principal office is located at 106-09 101st Ave, Ozone Park, NY 11416. Phone: (718) 850-0010. Email: info@gadurarealestate.com.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information you provide when you visit gadurarealestate.com (the "Site") or contact us through any form on the Site.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you submit a form on our Site (home valuation request, contact form, buyer inquiry), we collect:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (where requested)
  • Property address (for valuation requests)
  • Your message or inquiry details
  • Language preference

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Site, we may automatically collect standard technical data including your IP address, browser type, referring URL, and pages viewed. This data is used only for internal analytics and security purposes. We do not sell this data.

No Cookies for Advertising

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels. We do not share your browsing data with advertisers.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information you submit is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry about buying, selling, or renting a property
  • Prepare a Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) for your property
  • Schedule showings or consultations
  • Send you property listings or market updates you have requested
  • Contact you regarding the real estate services you inquired about

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Our licensed agents — Vinod K. Gadura (Broker), Gaurav Bhardwaj, and Nitin K. Gadura, for the purpose of responding to your inquiry
  • Formspree — Our form submission service processes form data to deliver it to us. Formspree is bound by its own Privacy Policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy
  • Legal requirements — If required by law, court order, or governmental authority

We do not share your data with mortgage companies, title companies, or other real estate vendors without your explicit consent.

5. Data Retention

We retain information you submit for as long as needed to fulfill the real estate transaction or inquiry, or as required by applicable law. Contact information for active clients is retained for a minimum of 3 years per NYS real estate record-keeping requirements.

6. Your Rights (New York Residents)

Under New York General Business Law §899-aa and related laws, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of receiving marketing communications from us at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: info@gadurarealestate.com or call (718) 850-0010.

7. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. Form submissions are transmitted via HTTPS. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, contact us immediately.

9. Third-Party Links

Our Site contains links to external sites (Zillow, Formspree, Google Maps, OpenStreetMap, OneKey MLS). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
