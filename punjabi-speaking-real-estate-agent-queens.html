The Multigenerational Home Advantage

Many Punjabi families prefer to buy homes that accommodate parents, adult children, and sometimes grandparents together. Queens 2-family and 3-family stock fits this perfectly — and FHA financing allows owner-occupant buyers to purchase up to 4-unit properties at 3.5% down. We structure these deals every week.
