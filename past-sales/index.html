What "Represented" Means

Every sale listed here was run under a written representation agreement — either a listing agreement (when we represented the seller) or a buyer-representation agreement (when we represented the buyer). Under New York Real Property Law Article 12-A, this representation carries specific fiduciary duties: loyalty, obedience, reasonable care, accounting, confidentiality, and full disclosure. No client, no sale.
