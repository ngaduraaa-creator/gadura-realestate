Neighborhood Overview

What makes Valley Stream 11580 distinctive

Zip code 11580 covers Valley Stream village and adjacent areas in the Town of Hempstead, Nassau County, directly bordering the Queens neighborhood of Springfield Gardens. Valley Stream is one of Nassau County’s most diverse communities — a genuine mix of Black, Caribbean-American, South Asian, Latin American, and white families who have created an authentically integrated community over several generations. The village center along Rockaway Avenue has restaurants, the Valley Stream State Park, and local shops. Residents frequently cross into Queens for work, shopping, and community, and many Queens buyers target Valley Stream for the combination of more space, a yard, and Nassau County school options at a price point not dramatically above southeast Queens.

Housing in 11580 is predominantly postwar Cape Cods, split-levels, and ranch homes on 40×100 to 60×100 lots, with some Colonials on larger lots in the more residential sections. Most homes have driveways and private rear yards. The LIRR Valley Stream station (Long Beach and Far Rockaway branches) provides service to Penn Station in approximately 25–30 minutes — one of the fastest LIRR commutes in Nassau County at this price point. Valley Stream State Park, with its pools, athletic fields, and picnic areas, is a genuine community asset. Nassau County property taxes are a meaningful ownership cost; buyers typically budget $8,000–$14,000 per year in this area.

Common Questions

FAQ — Buying & selling in 11580 Valley Stream

Why do Queens buyers often look at Valley Stream 11580?

Valley Stream is popular with buyers from southeast Queens because it offers more house for the money — detached homes with driveways and yards, typically on larger lots than comparable Queens properties — at a price point that is often not dramatically different from south Queens. The LIRR commute is faster than many bus-to-subway combinations from Queens, and Nassau County schools are a draw for families with children.

What are property taxes like in Valley Stream?

Annual property taxes in Valley Stream 11580 typically run $8,000–$14,000 per year depending on assessed value, the STAR exemption, and other applicable exemptions. Nassau County taxes are among the highest in the country; buyers should always request a current tax bill and factor taxes into their monthly carrying cost calculation before making an offer.

What are typical home prices in Valley Stream 11580?

In 2024–2025, homes in 11580 Valley Stream typically sell in the $600,000–$850,000 range. Updated Cape Cods and split-levels in good condition with modern kitchens and baths are at the higher end. Homes requiring updating can be found in the $600,000–$650,000 range and attract buyers willing to renovate.

How long is the LIRR commute from Valley Stream to Manhattan?

The LIRR Long Beach and Far Rockaway branches both serve Valley Stream station with direct service to Penn Station in approximately 25–32 minutes on peak trains. This is one of Nassau County’s fastest and most affordable commutes, and it is a key reason Valley Stream attracts buyers who work in Manhattan but want Nassau County living.
