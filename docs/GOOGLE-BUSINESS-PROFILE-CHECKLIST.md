## Part 4 — Reviews (the trust signal)

Google ranks businesses in the local pack heavily by review count and recency. Your current rating is 4.9★ with 57 reviews — excellent baseline.

### 4.1 — Weekly ask
After every closing, every open-house visit that converted to an offer, and every referral:
1. Text the client your Google review short link: `g.page/r/[your-ID]/review`
2. Say something like: *"If our work this week was helpful, a quick Google review means a lot. Takes 60 seconds. Here's the link."*
3. Don't script the review content — let them write honestly

### 4.2 — Respond to every review within 48 hours
- 5-star: short thank-you, reference something specific from their review
- 4-star and below: thank them, acknowledge the concern, offer to discuss offline (never argue publicly)
- Never ignore a review

### 4.3 — Request review removal
- For spam, fake, or clearly off-topic reviews: flag via the three-dot menu
- Google removes roughly 30% of flagged reviews

### 4.4 — Do not incentivize reviews
- No "leave a review and get $X" offers
- No "five-star or nothing" requests
- Both are against Google ToS and can result in profile suspension

## Red Flags That Will Hurt You

- **Duplicate profiles** — if Google shows multiple profiles for Gadura Real Estate at the same address, merge them or have duplicates removed
- **Keyword-stuffed name** — "Gadura Real Estate Queens Best Broker" gets penalized
- **Wrong address** — if you move offices, update IMMEDIATELY. Address mismatches tank ranking
- **Fake reviews** — asking friends to leave 5-star reviews without being real clients is detectable and gets profiles suspended
- **Review gating** — asking only happy clients for reviews violates Google policy
- **Stale profile** — no posts or photos in 90+ days drops you in local pack ranking
