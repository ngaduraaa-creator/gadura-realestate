How Jagman Works

Jagman’s Listing System — From Prep to Closing

Every listing goes through Jagman’s proven four-stage system. There are no shortcuts, no skipped steps, and no generic MLS uploads. This is why his sellers get more money in less time.

Stage 01 🛋️

Staging Strategy

Before a single photo is taken, Jagman conducts a full staging walkthrough. He advises on furniture arrangement, decluttering priorities, paint touch-ups, and curb appeal. For vacant homes, he coordinates professional staging furniture. A well-staged home photographs better, shows better, and sells for more — this is never optional in Jagman’s process.

Stage 02 📸

Professional Photography

Jagman works with professional real estate photographers — not smartphone cameras, not wide-angle distortions. Every listing gets HDR interior shots, exterior golden-hour photos, and where applicable, aerial overviews. Listings with professional photos receive significantly more online views and showings in the first 48 hours than those without.

Stage 03 📣

Aggressive Marketing

Jagman doesn’t just post to MLS and wait. He runs a multi-channel launch: MLS syndication across Zillow, Realtor.com, Trulia, and 200+ partner sites; Facebook and Instagram targeted ad campaigns; email campaigns to his active buyer database; and personal outreach to buyer agents with pre-qualified clients. Your listing gets seen by buyers, not just browsers.

Stage 04 📋

Offer Management & Closing

When offers come in — and Jagman’s listings typically receive multiple — he walks you through each one clearly: price, financing contingencies, inspection terms, closing timeline. He negotiates on your behalf with data and confidence, managing the process through to a successful closing while keeping you informed at every step.
