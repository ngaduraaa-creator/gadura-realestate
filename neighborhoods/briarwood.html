Why Briarwood?

Briarwood is Queens' best-kept affordable housing secret — co-ops under $350K, E/F express subway, and proximity to Jamaica's massive transit hub.

Local insight: Briarwood's Van Wyck Expressway provides direct highway access to JFK Airport in under 15 minutes — making it a top choice for airline industry workers and frequent travelers.

Home Prices in Briarwood (2026)

The median sale price in Briarwood is $325,000, up +2.8% year-over-year, based on data from Redfin / Zillow (co-op dominant). The typical price range runs from $300,000 to $350,000 depending on property type, condition, and block.

Source: Redfin / Zillow (co-op dominant). Data reflects median sale prices 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024.

Housing Stock Breakdown

Briarwood's housing stock is dominated by 1-family detached homes, making it popular with families seeking private outdoor space.
