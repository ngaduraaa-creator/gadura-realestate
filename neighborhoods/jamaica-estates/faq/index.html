Frequently Asked Questions About Jamaica Estates, Queens NY

Common questions from buyers, sellers, and renters about Jamaica Estates real estate

What is the average home price in Jamaica Estates, Queens?
The median home price in Jamaica Estates is $1,150,000 as of 2026-04, up +7.0% year-over-year. Prices range from $1,113,000 to $1,260,000 depending on property type and condition. Source: PropertyShark / Homes.com / RealtyTrac.
Is Jamaica Estates a good place to buy a home?
Jamaica Estates is considered an excellent value within Queens. Jamaica Estates offers Queens' most prestigious residential address — large Tudor and Colonial homes on elevated, curvilinear streets with gated-community character at NYC tax rates. With a 45-minute commute to Midtown Manhattan via Q30, Jamaica Estates offers significant lifestyle advantages at a fraction of the cost of closer-in neighborhoods.
What are property taxes like in Jamaica Estates?
Property taxes in Jamaica Estates are approximately $14,000/year for a median-priced home, reflecting an effective rate of 0.88%. This translates to roughly $1,166/month in tax escrow. Source: NYC Department of Finance FY2024–2025.
How long does it take to commute from Jamaica Estates to Manhattan?
The average commute from Jamaica Estates to Midtown Manhattan is approximately 45 minutes via Q30. Additional transit options include Q31, Q43.
What are the best schools in Jamaica Estates?
Public schools serving Jamaica Estates include: PS 131 Abigail Adams (GreatSchools 8/10), PS 212 (GreatSchools 7/10), Forest Hills High School (zoned) (GreatSchools 8/10). For the most current ratings and to verify exact school zones for a specific property, use the GreatSchools.org tool.
Is Jamaica Estates safe?
Jamaica Estates is a residential neighborhood in Queens. Crime statistics for Queens are published by the NYPD and can be reviewed by precinct at NYPD CompStat. As with all neighborhoods, safety varies by specific block and time of day. We encourage prospective buyers to visit in person at different times.
What ZIP codes does Jamaica Estates cover?
Jamaica Estates is served by ZIP code: 11432.
Are there investment properties available in Jamaica Estates?
Yes. Jamaica Estates's market includes a significant portion of 2-family homes — approximately 20% of the housing stock. These properties offer investors rental income potential of approximately $7,000/month combined. See our full Jamaica Estates investment guide.
How do I get first-time buyer assistance in Jamaica Estates?
First-time buyers in Jamaica Estates may qualify for NYC HPD HomeFirst (up to $100,000 in down payment assistance), SONYMA below-market rate mortgages, and FHA loans with 3.5% down. See our complete first-time buyer guide for Jamaica Estates.
What is Jamaica Estates known for?
Jamaica Estates was developed in 1907 as one of New York's first planned suburban communities — its distinctive curvilinear street layout was intentionally designed to discourage through traffic and preserve the neighborhood's estate character.
