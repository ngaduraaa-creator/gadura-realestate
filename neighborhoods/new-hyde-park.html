Why New Hyde Park?

Herricks School District is the reason families pay a premium for New Hyde Park. Ranked #23 in New York State with a 10/10 GreatSchools rating and 98% math proficiency, it's one of Long Island's finest.

Local insight: Herricks High School ranks #23 in New York State — one of only a handful of public schools in Nassau County to achieve a perfect 10/10 GreatSchools rating with 98% math proficiency.

Home Prices in New Hyde Park (2026)

The median sale price in New Hyde Park is $930,000, up +5.5% year-over-year, based on data from Redfin / RealtyTrac. The typical price range runs from $919,000 to $944,000 depending on property type, condition, and block.

Source: Redfin / RealtyTrac. Data reflects median sale prices 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024.
