Frequently Asked Questions About South Ozone Park, Queens NY

Common questions from buyers, sellers, and renters about South Ozone Park real estate

What is the average home price in South Ozone Park, Queens?
The median home price in South Ozone Park is $748,000 as of 2026-04, up +6.4% year-over-year. Prices range from $733,000 to $761,000 depending on property type and condition. Source: Rocket Homes / Zillow / PropertyShark.
Is South Ozone Park a good place to buy a home?
South Ozone Park is considered an excellent value within Queens. South Ozone Park offers newer housing stock than neighboring Woodhaven, JFK airport connectivity, and a tight-knit residential community — all below the Queens median price. With a 45-minute commute to Midtown Manhattan via A Train (Howard Beach–JFK, nearby), South Ozone Park offers significant lifestyle advantages at a fraction of the cost of closer-in neighborhoods.
What are property taxes like in South Ozone Park?
Property taxes in South Ozone Park are approximately $6,900/year for a median-priced home, reflecting an effective rate of 0.88%. This translates to roughly $575/month in tax escrow. Source: NYC Department of Finance FY2024–2025.
How long does it take to commute from South Ozone Park to Manhattan?
The average commute from South Ozone Park to Midtown Manhattan is approximately 45 minutes via A Train (Howard Beach–JFK, nearby). Additional transit options include Q7, Q10.
What are the best schools in South Ozone Park?
Public schools serving South Ozone Park include: PS 121 Queens (GreatSchools 6/10), PS 186 Castlewood (GreatSchools 6/10), John Adams High School (GreatSchools 4/10). For the most current ratings and to verify exact school zones for a specific property, use the GreatSchools.org tool.
Is South Ozone Park safe?
South Ozone Park is a residential neighborhood in Queens. Crime statistics for Queens are published by the NYPD and can be reviewed by precinct at NYPD CompStat. As with all neighborhoods, safety varies by specific block and time of day. We encourage prospective buyers to visit in person at different times.
What ZIP codes does South Ozone Park cover?
South Ozone Park is served by ZIP code: 11420.
Are there investment properties available in South Ozone Park?
Yes. South Ozone Park's market includes a significant portion of 2-family homes — approximately 55% of the housing stock. These properties offer investors rental income potential of approximately $6,000/month combined. See our full South Ozone Park investment guide.
How do I get first-time buyer assistance in South Ozone Park?
First-time buyers in South Ozone Park may qualify for NYC HPD HomeFirst (up to $100,000 in down payment assistance), SONYMA below-market rate mortgages, and FHA loans with 3.5% down. See our complete first-time buyer guide for South Ozone Park.
What is South Ozone Park known for?
South Ozone Park's ZIP code 11420 is one of the most densely residential zip codes in Queens, with nearly 77,000 residents packed into a compact footprint adjacent to JFK Airport.
