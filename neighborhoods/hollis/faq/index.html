Frequently Asked Questions About Hollis, Queens NY

Common questions from buyers, sellers, and renters about Hollis real estate

What is the average home price in Hollis, Queens?
The median home price in Hollis is $748,000 as of 2026-04, up +9.0% year-over-year. Prices range from $730,000 to $765,000 depending on property type and condition. Source: Homes.com / Rocket Homes.
Is Hollis a good place to buy a home?
Hollis is considered an excellent value within Queens. Hollis offers more single-family homes per block than any other SE Queens neighborhood — 43% detached 1-family homes with LIRR access and Nassau County borders. With a 38-minute commute to Midtown Manhattan via LIRR Hollis Station (Hempstead Branch), Hollis offers significant lifestyle advantages at a fraction of the cost of closer-in neighborhoods.
What are property taxes like in Hollis?
Property taxes in Hollis are approximately $6,900/year for a median-priced home, reflecting an effective rate of 0.88%. This translates to roughly $575/month in tax escrow. Source: NYC Department of Finance FY2024–2025.
How long does it take to commute from Hollis to Manhattan?
The average commute from Hollis to Midtown Manhattan is approximately 38 minutes via LIRR Hollis Station (Hempstead Branch). Additional transit options include Q2, Q3.
What are the best schools in Hollis?
Public schools serving Hollis include: PS 134 Hollis (GreatSchools 6/10), PS 131 Abigail Adams (adjacent) (GreatSchools 8/10), Andrew Jackson High School (GreatSchools 4/10). For the most current ratings and to verify exact school zones for a specific property, use the GreatSchools.org tool.
Is Hollis safe?
Hollis is a residential neighborhood in Queens. Crime statistics for Queens are published by the NYPD and can be reviewed by precinct at NYPD CompStat. As with all neighborhoods, safety varies by specific block and time of day. We encourage prospective buyers to visit in person at different times.
What ZIP codes does Hollis cover?
Hollis is served by ZIP code: 11423.
Are there investment properties available in Hollis?
Yes. Hollis's market includes a significant portion of 2-family homes — approximately 35% of the housing stock. These properties offer investors rental income potential of approximately $5,400/month combined. See our full Hollis investment guide.
How do I get first-time buyer assistance in Hollis?
First-time buyers in Hollis may qualify for NYC HPD HomeFirst (up to $100,000 in down payment assistance), SONYMA below-market rate mortgages, and FHA loans with 3.5% down. See our complete first-time buyer guide for Hollis.
What is Hollis known for?
Hollis holds a special place in American music history — the neighborhood is the birthplace of hip-hop legends Run-DMC, LL Cool J, and Ja Rule, all of whom grew up on Hollis Avenue.
