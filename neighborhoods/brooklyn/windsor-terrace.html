About Windsor Terrace Real Estate

Windsor Terrace is a charming, family-friendly neighborhood nestled between Prospect Park and Green-Wood Cemetery in Brooklyn. Known for its quiet, tree-lined streets, tight-knit community feel, and classic Brooklyn row houses, Windsor Terrace offers an intimate village atmosphere within New York City.

Windsor Terrace Real Estate Market Overview

Windsor Terrace features a mix of classic brick row houses, limestone townhouses, co-ops, and some newer condo developments. Living in Windsor Terrace

Windsor Terrace offers residents a peaceful residential experience with easy access to some of Brooklyn's best amenities:

  • Parks & Recreation: Direct access to Prospect Park's southwest corner, Green-Wood Cemetery's historic grounds, and neighborhood playgrounds
  • Shopping & Dining: Prospect Park West and Fort Hamilton Parkway offer local shops, cafes, and restaurants with a neighborhood feel
  • Transportation: F and G trains at Fort Hamilton Parkway and 15th Street-Prospect Park stations, easy access to Manhattan and downtown Brooklyn
  • Community: Active neighborhood associations, local library branch, family-oriented community events, and a strong sense of belonging
