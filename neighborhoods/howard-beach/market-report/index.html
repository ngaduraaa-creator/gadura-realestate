Housing Type Distribution

Understanding what type of homes sell in Howard Beach helps buyers and sellers set realistic expectations. The mix of 1-family, 2-family, and multi-family homes significantly affects price per square foot.

Market Conditions: Buyer or Seller?

With an average of 32 days on market, Howard Beach is currently a seller's market — homes move quickly and often receive multiple offers. Homes priced correctly in Howard Beach typically sell within 3–4 weeks.

What this means for buyers: Come pre-approved and be ready to move fast. Contingency-heavy offers are frequently passed over in favor of cleaner bids.

What this means for sellers: Pricing at market value (not above it) yields the fastest sale and best net proceeds. Overpricing by 5% typically adds 3–4 weeks to your timeline and a price reduction.

Price Per Square Foot

In Howard Beach, well-maintained 1-family homes trade at approximately $464–$542 per square foot. 2-family homes — valued on both living quality and rental income — often command a premium on a price-per-unit basis.

Rental Market Overview

For investors, Howard Beach 1-family homes rent for approximately $3,400/month. 2-family homes can generate combined rents of $5,600/month, helping offset mortgage and tax costs significantly.
