Frequently Asked Questions About Briarwood, Queens NY

Common questions from buyers, sellers, and renters about Briarwood real estate

What is the average home price in Briarwood, Queens?
The median home price in Briarwood is $325,000 as of 2026-04, up +2.8% year-over-year. Prices range from $300,000 to $350,000 depending on property type and condition. Source: Redfin / Zillow (co-op dominant).
Is Briarwood a good place to buy a home?
Briarwood is considered an excellent value within Queens. Briarwood is Queens' best-kept affordable housing secret — co-ops under $350K, E/F express subway, and proximity to Jamaica's massive transit hub. With a 30-minute commute to Midtown Manhattan via E/F Subway (Briarwood/Van Wyck Blvd), Briarwood offers significant lifestyle advantages at a fraction of the cost of closer-in neighborhoods.
What are property taxes like in Briarwood?
Property taxes in Briarwood are approximately $6,600/year for a median-priced home, reflecting an effective rate of 0.88%. This translates to roughly $550/month in tax escrow. Source: NYC Department of Finance FY2024–2025.
How long does it take to commute from Briarwood to Manhattan?
The average commute from Briarwood to Midtown Manhattan is approximately 30 minutes via E/F Subway (Briarwood/Van Wyck Blvd). Additional transit options include Q20, Q44-SBS.
What are the best schools in Briarwood?
Public schools serving Briarwood include: PS 117 J. Woodbridge Davis (GreatSchools 6/10), PS 99 Kew Gardens (adjacent) (GreatSchools 8/10), Forest Hills High School (GreatSchools 8/10). For the most current ratings and to verify exact school zones for a specific property, use the GreatSchools.org tool.
Is Briarwood safe?
Briarwood is a residential neighborhood in Queens. Crime statistics for Queens are published by the NYPD and can be reviewed by precinct at NYPD CompStat. As with all neighborhoods, safety varies by specific block and time of day. We encourage prospective buyers to visit in person at different times.
What ZIP codes does Briarwood cover?
Briarwood is served by ZIP code: 11435.
Are there investment properties available in Briarwood?
Yes. Briarwood's market includes a significant portion of 2-family homes — approximately 12% of the housing stock. These properties offer investors rental income potential of approximately $0/month combined. See our full Briarwood investment guide.
How do I get first-time buyer assistance in Briarwood?
First-time buyers in Briarwood may qualify for NYC HPD HomeFirst (up to $100,000 in down payment assistance), SONYMA below-market rate mortgages, and FHA loans with 3.5% down. See our complete first-time buyer guide for Briarwood.
What is Briarwood known for?
Briarwood's Van Wyck Expressway provides direct highway access to JFK Airport in under 15 minutes — making it a top choice for airline industry workers and frequent travelers.
