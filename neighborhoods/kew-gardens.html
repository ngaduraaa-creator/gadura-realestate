Why Kew Gardens?

Kew Gardens offers something rare in Queens — pre-war co-op apartments under $350K with E/F express access to Manhattan in 28 minutes, zoned for the 8/10-rated PS 99.

Local insight: PS 99 Kew Gardens earns an 8/10 GreatSchools rating with 76% math proficiency — making it one of the highest-rated elementary schools in all of SE Queens.

Home Prices in Kew Gardens (2026)

The median sale price in Kew Gardens is $325,000, up +3.1% year-over-year, based on data from Redfin / Rocket Homes (co-op/condo dominant). The typical price range runs from $300,000 to $349,000 depending on property type, condition, and block.

Source: Redfin / Rocket Homes (co-op/condo dominant). Data reflects median sale prices 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024.
