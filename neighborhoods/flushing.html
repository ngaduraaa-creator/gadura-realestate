Living in Flushing

Flushing offers residents an exceptional quality of life with numerous amenities:

  • Parks & Recreation: Flushing Meadows-Corona Park, home of the 1964 World's Fair
  • Shopping & Entertainment: Main Street and Roosevelt Avenue bustling commercial districts
  • Transportation: 7 subway line and LIRR Port Washington Branch
  • Dining: World-renowned Asian cuisine and the famous Flushing food courts
