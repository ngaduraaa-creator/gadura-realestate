Living in Long Island City

Long Island City offers residents an exceptional quality of life with numerous amenities:

  • Parks & Recreation: MoMA PS1, Gantry Plaza State Park, and Hunters Point waterfront
  • Shopping & Entertainment: Court Square, Vernon Boulevard, and the growing retail corridors
  • Transportation: 7, E, M, G subway lines with direct Manhattan access
  • Dining: A growing food scene with acclaimed restaurants and craft breweries
