Is it a buyer's market or a seller's market?

The law of supply and demand sets the price of every home — and right now, across most of Queens & Long Island, low inventory continues to favor sellers. But "the market" is really hundreds of micro-markets: Richmond Hill prices behave nothing like Jamaica Estates or Valley Stream. We track buyer demand block by block and price your home to capture it.

What an online estimate misses

Automated valuations pull from public records and recent sales, then apply a formula. They can't account for a renovated kitchen, a finished basement, a legal two-family conversion, a premium lot, or a bidding war two doors down. That gap is exactly where a local broker adds — and protects — real dollars.
