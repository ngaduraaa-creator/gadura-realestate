Who We Are

Built in Queens, For Queens

In 2006, Vinod K. Gadura opened Gadura Real Estate LLC on 101st Avenue in Ozone Park. He had one goal: to give the working families of South Queens a real estate agency that understood them — their culture, their languages, their needs, and their dreams.

At the time, South Queens was underserved by large brokerages that didn't speak Hindi or Punjabi, didn't understand the multi-family market that drives the neighborhood, and treated clients like numbers rather than people. Vinod built something different.

Twenty years later, the Gadura family continues to operate from the same office, serve the same neighborhoods, and hold to the same principle: every client who walks through our door is treated like family. Today, Vinod's son Nitin and associate Gaurav Bhardwaj carry that mission forward with the same integrity and dedication.

Our Journey

20 Years of Milestones

2006

Gadura Real Estate LLC Founded

Vinod K. Gadura opens the doors at 106-09 101st Avenue, Ozone Park. The office serves the growing South Asian and Caribbean communities of South Queens. First listing: a two-family home on Rockaway Blvd.

2008

Navigating the Financial Crisis

While many agencies closed, Gadura Real Estate stayed open — counseling families through foreclosure prevention, short sales, and market uncertainty. Trusted guidance when it mattered most.

2012

$10M in Annual Transactions

After steady growth through the recovery years, the agency surpasses $10 million in annual real estate transactions. The reputation for honest, community-focused service brings referrals from across Queens and Long Island.

2016

Expanding Into Long Island

Growing client demand leads the team to expand services into Nassau County — Valley Stream, Elmont, Hempstead, and New Hyde Park. Many Queens families buying their first home in the suburbs trust Gadura to guide them.

2019

Gaurav Bhardwaj Joins the Team

Licensed agent Gaurav Bhardwaj joins Gadura Real Estate, bringing additional capacity, energy, and language skills. The agency's footprint in Richmond Hill and Jamaica expands significantly.

2021

Nitin K. Gadura Gets Licensed

Vinod's son Nitin earns his NYS real estate salesperson license and joins the family business. He brings digital marketing expertise, a younger client network, and deep community roots from growing up in Ozone Park.

2024

$100M+ in Lifetime Transactions

Gadura Real Estate surpasses $100 million in lifetime real estate transactions — a milestone built entirely on word-of-mouth, referrals, and the trust of the Queens and Long Island communities.

Today

Still Family. Still Community.

The same office, the same phone number, the same values. Three generations of commitment to the families of Queens and Long Island. Whatever the market brings next, we'll face it together — as family.

Our Community

Serving Every Family in Queens

Queens is one of the most diverse places on earth. Our team speaks your language and understands your culture — because we live here too.

🇮🇳

Indian & South Asian Community

Hindi, Punjabi, and Urdu spoken. Deep expertise in the South Asian corridors of Ozone Park, Richmond Hill, Jamaica, and Long Island. We understand joint family home searches, multi-family investments, and cultural priorities.

🇬🇾

Guyanese & Caribbean Community

South Ozone Park, Richmond Hill, and Flatbush are home to one of the largest Guyanese communities in the US. We've helped hundreds of Indo-Guyanese and Afro-Caribbean families buy, sell, and invest in Queens real estate.

🇧🇩

Bengali Community

Jamaica and Jamaica Hills have a thriving Bangladeshi community. We work closely with Bengali families navigating home ownership for the first time, understanding the specific legal and financial considerations they face.

🇪🇸

Hispanic & Latino Community

Spanish spoken. Jackson Heights, Elmhurst, Woodhaven, and South Ozone Park have strong Latino communities. Nitin speaks conversational Spanish and our team is committed to serving Latino families throughout Queens.

✊🏾

Black & African American Community

Jamaica, Hollis, St. Albans, and Springfield Gardens have deep Black community roots. We are committed to equitable housing access and take pride in helping Black families build wealth through real estate in Queens and Long Island.

👴👵

Elderly & Senior Clients

Our office is accessible and our communication is clear, patient, and in plain language. We take extra time with elderly clients, explain every document in full, and never rush. Because your lifetime of work deserves our full respect.
