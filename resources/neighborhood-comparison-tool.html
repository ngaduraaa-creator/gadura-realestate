How to Use This Comparison

Every buyer has different priorities. Here is how to use this data effectively:

  • Budget-first buyers: Sort mentally by median price. East New York ($520K), Elmont ($530K), Valley Stream ($560K), and Canarsie ($580K) offer the lowest entry points across our service area.
  • Commute-driven buyers: Long Island City (10-15 min) and Williamsburg (15-20 min) offer the shortest commutes. Forest Hills and Astoria balance commute time with neighborhood quality.
  • School-focused families: Forest Hills, Bayside, Garden City, and Great Neck consistently rank among the top school districts. Expect to pay a 15-25% premium for top-rated school zones.
  • Investors: Jamaica, Ozone Park, Richmond Hill, and East New York offer the strongest rental yields and fastest appreciation. Focus on multi-family properties for maximum cash flow. See our Multi-Family Homes page.
  • Lifestyle buyers: Astoria for dining and culture, Park Slope for family life with urban amenities, Williamsburg for nightlife and creativity, Bay Ridge for waterfront and community.
