## Recording Tips

- Record in a quiet room with no echo
- Smile while recording -- it makes your voice warmer
- Speak at a moderate pace, slightly slower than normal conversation
- Pronounce your name and the company name clearly
- Keep each greeting under 30 seconds (callers hang up on long voicemails)
- Record 2-3 takes and pick the best one
- Listen back on a different device to check audio quality

## Dialpad Configuration Steps

### Setting Up Voicemail Greetings

1. Open Dialpad app or web portal
2. Go to Settings > Voicemail
3. For each greeting:
   - Tap/click "Record" or "Upload"
   - If recording: read the script aloud, listen back, re-record if needed
   - If uploading: record as a voice memo on your phone, then upload the file
4. Assign each greeting to its time slot:
   - Business hours greeting: set as default
   - After-hours greeting: set as after-hours/off-hours greeting
   - Holiday greeting: manually swap in before each holiday

### Setting Up Text-Back Auto-Replies

1. Go to Settings > Call Handling or Settings > Auto-Reply
2. Enable "Send text when call is missed"
3. Enter the appropriate text-back message
4. Configure different messages for business hours vs. after hours if supported
5. Test by calling your Dialpad number and not answering

### Holiday Greeting Swap Process

Before each holiday:
1. Go to Settings > Voicemail
2. Change the active greeting to the holiday version
3. After the holiday: swap back to the regular business hours and after-hours greetings
4. Consider recording holiday-specific versions (Thanksgiving, Christmas) in advance so you can swap with one tap
