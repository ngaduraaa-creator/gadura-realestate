Vinod K. Gadura

Licensed Real Estate Broker · New York State

OneKey® MLS Member · Gadura Real Estate LLC

Vinod Gadura founded Gadura Real Estate LLC in Ozone Park in 2006 with a simple mission: to give the Queens community a trusted, family-run real estate agency they could count on. Twenty years later, that mission hasn't changed.

With deep roots in the South Asian and Caribbean communities of Queens, Vinod brings not just real estate expertise, but cultural understanding and genuine care to every transaction. He has personally overseen more than $60 million in real estate transactions across Ozone Park, Richmond Hill, Jamaica, Woodhaven, and South Ozone Park.

Vinod is especially sought after for complex multi-family and mixed-use transactions. His reputation for honest pricing, strong negotiation, and patient guidance has made him one of the most trusted names in South Queens real estate.

Gaurav Bhardwaj joined Gadura Real Estate with a passion for helping families find homes that fit their lives. He specializes in first-time buyers and seller clients throughout Queens, bringing energy, attention to detail, and a genuine desire to make the process as smooth as possible.

Gaurav is known for his responsiveness — clients consistently praise his availability and willingness to go the extra mile, whether that's visiting properties on short notice, walking buyers through the entire mortgage and closing process, or negotiating on their behalf at critical moments.

He has strong ties to the South Asian community in Queens and Long Island, and works extensively with Indian and Guyanese families navigating the New York real estate market for the first time or expanding their property portfolio.

Our Philosophy

Why We're Different

We are a family-owned real estate agency that has served our community for over 20 years. Unlike the big corporate entities — when you come here, we treat you like family.

That means honest advice even when it's not what you want to hear. It means being available when you call, returning your messages, and not vanishing after the commission is collected. It means speaking your language — Hindi, Punjabi, Spanish, Creole — so nothing gets lost in translation.

It means our name is on this business, and we intend to keep it worth something.

🏠

Community First

We serve Indian, Guyanese, Bengali, Caribbean, Hispanic, and Black families with equal dedication. Your community is our community.

🤝

No Pressure, Ever

We will never rush you into a decision. We'd rather lose a sale than have a client regret a transaction they felt pushed into.

Always Reachable

We answer calls. We return messages. When you're working through one of the biggest decisions of your life, you deserve to be able to reach your agent.
