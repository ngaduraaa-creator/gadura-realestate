About Vinod Gadura

The Broker Who Built Gadura Real Estate From the Ground Up

Vinod Gadura opened Gadura Real Estate LLC in 2006 — not as a franchise location, but as a independent brokerage rooted in the Queens community he grew up in. Nearly two decades later, that decision defines everything about how the firm operates: local-first, relationship-driven, and fiercely accountable to every client.

"I have been servicing the New York market for over 20 years with expert-level experience in both Commercial and Residential sales and negotiation."

Vinod handles all aspects of a transaction from first conversation to closing table — and he brings that hands-on approach to every deal the brokerage takes on. His team of 17 licensed agents operates under his direct supervision, meaning every client benefits from his 28 years of experience regardless of which agent they work with.
