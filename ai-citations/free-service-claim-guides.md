## 5. Yelp for Business (feeds Apple Maps + Siri + Bing)

**URL:** https://biz.yelp.com/
**Why:** Yelp data flows downstream to Apple, Bing, and is heavily cited in AI Overviews + Perplexity local answers.

**Steps:**
1. Go to https://biz.yelp.com/ → search for the business
2. If listing exists (it probably does — Yelp scrapes phone books): click "Claim this business"
3. Verify by phone (instant)
4. Complete profile: hours, services, photos (10+), about, languages, attributes
5. Pin 3 specialty services as featured
6. Respond to every existing review (positive AND negative — Yelp ranks active responders)

**URL:** https://www.bbb.org/get-listed

**Steps:**
1. Go to https://www.bbb.org/get-listed
2. Free non-accredited listing first (instant)
3. Optional: pay for accreditation (~$500/year — gets the "BBB Accredited" badge that adds Google trust)
4. Add full profile: hours, services, languages, NYS license #10991238487
5. Add 3+ photos
