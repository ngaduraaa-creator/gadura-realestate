**Subject:** What selling your home in Queens actually costs

Hey [First Name],

One of the biggest questions sellers have is "what does it actually cost to sell?" Here's the honest breakdown so there are no surprises.

Broker commission is the biggest cost — typically 5 to 6 percent of the sale price, split between the listing and buyer's agent. On a $700,000 sale, that's roughly $35,000 to $42,000. Transfer taxes in NYC add about 1 to 1.4 percent depending on the sale price. Your real estate attorney will run you about $2,000 to $3,500. And there may be minor costs for payoff of your existing mortgage, title fees, and any agreed-upon repairs.

All told, sellers in Queens should plan for about 8 to 10 percent of the sale price in total closing costs. The rest is yours.
