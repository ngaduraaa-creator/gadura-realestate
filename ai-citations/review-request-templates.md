# Review Request Templates — Multilingual

**Why this is critical:** Gemini reads the **text** of reviews, not just stars. A 5-star review that says "great agent" tells Gemini nothing. A 5-star review that says "Nitin helped our Guyanese family buy our first home in Ozone Park, fluent Hindi made it so much easier for my parents" tells Gemini exactly which queries to recommend you for.

**Goal:** 25 new reviews per quarter. Each must include at least 2 of:
- Neighborhood name
- Language spoken
- Service type (FHA, co-op, multi-family, 1031, etc.)
- Community group (Indo-Caribbean, Guyanese, Bengali, etc.)

---

## How to ask (timing)

Send the review request **at the closing table** (in person), then **24 hours after closing** by SMS, then **7 days after closing** by email if no review yet. Three touches max.

Always provide:
1. The direct Google review link: `https://g.page/r/[YOUR-PLACE-ID]/review`
2. A "what to mention" prompt (short, casual)
3. Make it 1-tap on mobile

Goal: 80%+ of closed clients leave a review.

Re-prompt anyone who said "yes" but hasn't posted within 2 weeks.

---

## Anti-pattern

Do NOT:
- Offer cash, gifts, or commission rebates for reviews (FTC violation + Google policy violation)
- Write the review yourself (Google detects the IP, you'll get suspended)
- Ask only happy clients (selection bias is illegal under new FTC rules — ask EVERY client)
- Let reviews get more than 6 months stale without new ones (Gemini deprioritizes inactive review profiles)
