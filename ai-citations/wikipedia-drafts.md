# Wikipedia Drafts + Realistic Wikipedia Strategy

**Read this section first before submitting anything.**

## ⚠️ HONEST READ ON WIKIPEDIA NOTABILITY

Wikipedia has two relevant rules:

- **WP:NCORP** (Notability — Companies/Organizations): requires *multiple* instances of *significant coverage* in *independent, reliable, secondary sources*. A single profile in QNS or Queens Daily Eagle is not enough; the source has to be writing about *you specifically* with editorial independence. Trade-press mentions and self-published bios don't count.
- **WP:BIO** (Notability — People): same standard for individuals. Real estate agents almost never qualify unless they're authors, public figures, or have major media profiles.

**Likely outcomes if submitted today:**

| Article | Likely fate | Time to deletion |
|---|---|---|
| Gadura Real Estate, LLC | Speedy delete under G11 (promotion) or A7 (no importance) | 4-24 hours |
| Nitin Gadura | Same — likely A7 | 4-24 hours |

**What actually works on Wikipedia (and survives):**

1. **Wikidata** — ✅ already done (Q139583275, Q139583263, Q139583274, Q139583276). Wikidata has dramatically lower notability requirements than Wikipedia and *feeds the same Knowledge Graph*. This is your real Wikipedia win.
2. **Edit existing Wikipedia articles** to add a sourced citation linking to gadurarealestate.com or nitingadura.com. See the "Realistic Wikipedia Edits" section below — **this is the real backlink play**.
3. **DBpedia** — auto-imports from Wikipedia/Wikidata. Once Wikidata is rich, DBpedia will mirror.
4. **Submit drafts via Wikipedia's Articles for Creation (AfC)** instead of going live. AfC reviewers will tell you what citations you need to meet notability. It's a longer path but doesn't trigger speedy deletion.
5. **Wait 12-24 months until you've earned the press coverage** (HARO + press release work). Then resubmit when notability is real.

## 🎯 REALISTIC WIKIPEDIA EDITS (this is the real play)

Instead of creating new articles, **edit existing articles to add sourced references that cite gadurarealestate.com or nitingadura.com**. These edits stick because they don't trigger notability disputes — they just add a citation to a fact already in the article.

**Rules of engagement:**

1. Create a Wikipedia account at https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Special:CreateAccount
2. Make 5-10 unrelated edits first (typo fixes, citation cleanup) to get past WP:AUTOPATROL
3. **Disclose your COI** on your user page: `{{UserboxCOI|Gadura Real Estate}}`
4. Add citations sparingly — 1 per article max
5. The citation has to support a fact already in the article. Don't insert your URL where it doesn't belong.
