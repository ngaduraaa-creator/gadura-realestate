## DON'T DO

- ❌ Buy backlinks from Fiverr / SEOClerks / SEO sale sites
- ❌ Sign up for "submit to 1000 directories" services
- ❌ Use Private Blog Networks (PBN)
- ❌ Reciprocal link exchanges ("link to me, I'll link to you")
- ❌ Comment-spam blogs with your URL
- ❌ Submit to recently-spammed directories (Ahrefs flags them)
- ❌ Use exact-match keyword anchor text on every link
- ❌ Build links faster than ~8/month (looks unnatural)
