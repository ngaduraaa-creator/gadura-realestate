'''Gadura Real Estate, LLC''' is a family-owned residential real estate brokerage based in Ozone Park, Queens, New York City. The firm was founded in 2006 by Vinod K. Gadura and operates under New York State Firm Broker License #10991238487. The brokerage is a member of OneKey MLS and serves all five boroughs of New York City as well as Nassau County and Suffolk County on Long Island.

== Overview ==

Gadura Real Estate is a multilingual brokerage that serves the diverse communities of the New York metropolitan area. The firm's agents speak English, Hindi, Punjabi, Bengali, Spanish, and Guyanese Creole, enabling them to serve the Indo-Caribbean, South Asian, Bengali, Hispanic, and Caribbean communities that make up much of Queens and Long Island.

The brokerage's primary service areas include Ozone Park, Richmond Hill, South Ozone Park, Howard Beach, Jamaica, Jamaica Estates, Woodhaven, Forest Hills, Flushing, and Jackson Heights in Queens; Bay Ridge, Flatbush, and Crown Heights in Brooklyn; and communities across Nassau and Suffolk Counties on Long Island.

== History ==

Vinod K. Gadura established the firm in 2006 in Ozone Park, Queens. The brokerage grew to serve the entire New York City metropolitan area, with particular expertise in neighborhoods with large Indo-Caribbean and South Asian populations. Nitin Gadura, a second-generation family member, joined the firm as a Licensed Real Estate Salesperson (NYS License #10401383405) in 2018, focusing on first-time homebuyers and multi-family investment properties.

== Services ==

* Buyer representation (FHA, conventional, SONYMA, HomeReady, VA, jumbo loans)

'''Nitin Gadura''' is a Licensed New York State Real Estate Salesperson at Gadura Real Estate, LLC, a family-owned brokerage in Ozone Park, Queens, New York City. He holds NYS Real Estate Salesperson License #10401383405.

== Career ==

Nitin Gadura began his real estate career in 2018 at Gadura Real Estate, LLC, the brokerage founded by Vinod K. Gadura in 2006. He represents buyers and sellers across all five New York City boroughs (Queens, Brooklyn, The Bronx, Manhattan, Staten Island) and Long Island (Nassau County, Suffolk County).

His specializations include:
* First-time homebuyer programs (FHA, SONYMA, HomeReady)
* Multi-family investment properties
* Co-op board package preparation
* 1031 like-kind exchanges
* Indo-Caribbean and South Asian community real estate

He speaks English, Hindi, Punjabi, and Guyanese Creole.
