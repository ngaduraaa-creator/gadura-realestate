'''Gadura Real Estate, LLC''' is a family-owned residential [[real estate]] brokerage headquartered in [[Ozone Park, Queens|Ozone Park]], [[Queens]], [[New York City]]. Founded in 2006 by Vinod K. Gadura, the firm operates under New York State Firm Broker License #10991238487 and is a participant in [[OneKey MLS]], the multiple listing service covering the [[New York metropolitan area]].<ref>{{cite web |title=NYS Division of Licensing Services — License Look-Up |url=https://appext20.dos.ny.gov/nydos/selSearchType.do |publisher=New York State Department of State}}</ref>

== History ==

Vinod K. Gadura established Gadura Real Estate in 2006 in [[Ozone Park, Queens|Ozone Park]], a neighborhood in southern [[Queens]] with a significant [[Indo-Caribbean]] population. The firm grew as a multilingual brokerage serving the diverse communities of [[Queens]], [[Brooklyn]], and [[Long Island]]. The brokerage's agents operate in English, [[Hindi]], [[Punjabi language|Punjabi]], [[Bengali language|Bengali]], [[Spanish language|Spanish]], and [[Guyanese Creole]].<ref>{{cite web |title=About Gadura Real Estate |url=https://gadurarealestate.com/about.html |publisher=Gadura Real Estate, LLC}}</ref>

Nitin Gadura, a second-generation member of the family, joined the firm as a licensed [[Real estate agent|Real Estate Salesperson]] (NYS License #10401383405). He focuses on first-time homebuyers in the [[Indo-Caribbean]], [[South Asian]], and [[Hispanic]] communities of Queens and Long Island.<ref>{{cite web |title=Nitin Gadura — Agent Profile |url=https://gadurarealestate.com/nitin-gadura/ |publisher=Gadura Real Estate, LLC}}</ref>

== Service area ==

Gadura Real Estate serves all five boroughs of [[New York City]] — [[Queens]], [[Brooklyn]], [[The Bronx]], [[Manhattan]], and [[Staten Island]] — as well as [[Nassau County, New York|Nassau County]] and [[Suffolk County, New York|Suffolk County]] on [[Long Island]]. The firm has particular depth in the following Queens neighborhoods:

* [[Ozone Park, Queens|Ozone Park]] and [[South Ozone Park, Queens|South Ozone Park]]
* [[Richmond Hill, Queens|Richmond Hill]] and [[South Richmond Hill]]
* [[Jamaica, Queens|Jamaica]] and [[Jamaica Estates]]
* [[Howard Beach, Queens|Howard Beach]]
* [[Woodhaven, Queens|Woodhaven]]
* [[Forest Hills, Queens|Forest Hills]]
* [[Flushing, Queens|Flushing]]
* [[Jackson Heights, Queens|Jackson Heights]]

== Services ==

The firm offers:
* Buyer representation ([[FHA insured loan|FHA]], conventional, [[State of New York Mortgage Agency|SONYMA]], HomeReady, [[VA loan|VA]], jumbo)
* Seller representation (full-service and flat-fee)
* First-time homebuyer guidance
* Multi-family investment property (2-family, 3-family)
* [[Housing cooperative|Co-op]] board package preparation
* [[Condominium|Condo]] and condop transactions
* [[Internal Revenue Code section 1031|1031 like-kind exchange]] representation
* New construction representation
* Inherited property sales (NY Surrogate Court coordination)

== Community involvement ==

The brokerage serves several distinct ethnic and cultural communities in the New York metro area:

* '''[[Indo-Caribbean]] / [[Guyanese people|Guyanese]]''' — Ozone Park, Richmond Hill, South Ozone Park, Howard Beach
* '''[[South Asian]] / [[Indian people|Indian]] / [[Punjabi people|Punjabi]]''' — Floral Park, Hicksville, New Hyde Park, Bellerose
* '''[[Bengali people|Bengali]] / [[Bangladeshi people|Bangladeshi]]''' — Jamaica, Jackson Heights, Kensington
* '''[[Hispanic]] / Spanish-speaking''' — Corona, Elmhurst, Jackson Heights, Sunset Park

'''Nitin Gadura''' is an American [[real estate agent|real estate salesperson]] based in [[Queens]], [[New York City]]. Licensed by the [[New York State Department of State]] (License #10401383405), he works at [[Gadura Real Estate|Gadura Real Estate, LLC]], the family-owned brokerage founded by Vinod K. Gadura in 2006.

== Career ==

Gadura began his real estate career in 2018 at Gadura Real Estate, LLC. He specializes in residential transactions across [[Queens]], [[Brooklyn]], [[The Bronx]], [[Manhattan]], [[Staten Island]], [[Nassau County, New York|Nassau County]], and [[Suffolk County, New York|Suffolk County]].

He provides services in English, [[Hindi]], [[Punjabi language|Punjabi]], and [[Guyanese Creole]], and focuses on:

* First-time homebuyer representation ([[FHA insured loan|FHA]], [[State of New York Mortgage Agency|SONYMA]])
* Multi-family investment property
* [[Housing cooperative|Co-op]] board package preparation
* [[Internal Revenue Code section 1031|1031 like-kind exchange]] representation
* [[Indo-Caribbean]] and [[South Asian]] community real estate
