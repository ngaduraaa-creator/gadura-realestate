==============================================================
BETTER BUSINESS BUREAU (BBB) — CREATE LISTING
==============================================================

WHY THIS MATTERS: BBB listings appear prominently in Google
Knowledge Panels and are cited by AI engines as trust signals.
A BBB-accredited business gets a "BBB Rating" badge that
significantly boosts AI trust scores.

STEP-BY-STEP:
1. Go to https://www.bbb.org/get-listed
   OR https://www.bbb.org/request-a-quote
2. Click "Get Your Free BBB Business Profile"
   (note: basic listing is FREE, accreditation is paid ~$500/yr)
3. Fill out the application with info below
4. BBB will verify your business via phone and public records

NOTE ON ACCREDITATION:
