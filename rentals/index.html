What You Need to Rent an Apartment in Queens

  • Income proof: Pay stubs or tax returns showing income of at least 40x the monthly rent (or a qualified guarantor)
  • Photo ID: Government-issued photo identification (driver's license, passport, or state ID)
  • Credit report: Most Queens landlords prefer a 650+ credit score, though options exist for lower scores
  • Employment letter: Verification of current employment on company letterhead
  • References: Contact information for one or two previous landlords
  • Security deposit: Typically one month's rent, paid at lease signing
  • Broker fee: If applicable; ask Nitin about no-fee rental options
