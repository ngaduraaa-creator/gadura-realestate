Good To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find the best real estate agent in Queens, NY?+
Look for a licensed agent with deep neighborhood knowledge, strong reviews and experience with your property type. The Gadura Real Estate team specializes in Ozone Park, Richmond Hill, Jamaica and Howard Beach — plus Nassau County — speaks Hindi, Punjabi, Bengali, Spanish and English, and has helped 500+ families since 2006.
How much is my house worth in Queens right now?+
Queens home values vary widely by neighborhood — as of 2026, roughly $580K in Jamaica to $735K+ in Richmond Hill and $850K+ in Howard Beach. The best way to know your exact value is a free Comparative Market Analysis based on recent comparable sales. Get your free valuation →
What are the closing costs when selling in Queens, NY?+
Sellers in Queens typically pay 8–10% of the sale price, including broker commission, NYC/NYS transfer taxes (1.4–1.825%), attorney fees ($2,000–$3,500) and any outstanding liens. Homes over $1M carry an additional mansion tax.
Is now a good time to buy a home in Queens?+
Queens remains one of NYC's strongest markets, appreciating 5–7% year-over-year in most neighborhoods. First-time buyers can access SONYMA programs with as little as 3% down, plus down-payment assistance.
Do you help with inherited properties and probate sales?+
Yes — we specialize in inherited property and estate sales across Queens and Long Island, guiding families through probate timelines, stepped-up basis tax benefits, property clean-out coordination and pricing strategy.
What neighborhoods do you serve?+
All of Queens — Ozone Park, Richmond Hill, Jamaica, Howard Beach, Woodhaven, South Ozone Park, Kew Gardens, Forest Hills, Astoria, Flushing and more — plus Long Island: Elmont, Floral Park, Valley Stream, Baldwin, Freeport and across Nassau County. See all neighborhoods →
